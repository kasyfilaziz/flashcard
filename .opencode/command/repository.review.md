**Objective:** Perform a comprehensive review of everything written in the last conversation.

**Instructions:**

1. **Retrieve & Analyze:** Access the complete transcript/context from the previous conversation.

2. **Use Sub-Agent:** Delegate the review task to the `@general` sub-agent to optimize context window usage and ensure focused analysis.

3. **Review Criteria:**
   - **Accuracy:** Verify all factual claims, data, and information presented
   - **Completeness:** Check if all aspects of the topic were adequately covered
   - **Clarity:** Evaluate if responses were clear, well-structured, and easy to understand
   - **Consistency:** Identify any contradictions or conflicting statements
   - **Tone & Style:** Assess professionalism, helpfulness, and communication quality
   - **Actionability:** Confirm whether requested tasks were properly addressed
   - **Potential Improvements:** Note areas that could have been better handled

4. **Output Format:** Provide:
   - Summary of the conversation topic
   - Key strengths identified
   - Issues or errors found
   - Specific recommendations for improvement
   - Overall assessment
